# Vitaboom

## Summary
B2B supplement/nutraceutical platform for clinics. Clinicians prescribe protocols (supplement regimens) to patients, who can order products through the platform. Includes dropshipping, subscriptions, commissions, clinic branding, and vendor/supplier integrations. MVP dropshipping solution is the current priority.

## Domain Concepts
- **Clinic** — Healthcare practice that prescribes supplements
- **Clinician** — Healthcare provider within a clinic
- **Patient** — End user who receives protocols and orders products
- **Protocol** — Prescribed supplement regimen (set of products + dosing)
- **Product** — Supplement/nutraceutical (sourced from vendors)
- **Order** — Patient purchase (direct or dropshipped)
- **Subscription** — Recurring product orders
- **Commission** — Clinic earnings from patient purchases
- **Vendor** — Supplement supplier (Shopify-based)
